What is Special Announcement Schema?

June 2026 · 424 words

SpecialAnnouncement schema is a type of structured data designed for time-sensitive announcements from organizations. It was introduced during the COVID-19 pandemic to help organizations communicate important public health information. While it was created for pandemic responses, SpecialAnnouncement schema can be used for any urgent or important announcement that affects the public, such as emergency closures, service changes, or safety notices.

How SpecialAnnouncement Schema Works

SpecialAnnouncement schema uses properties to describe the nature, date, and scope of an announcement. The announcement type category classifies the announcement such as school closures, travel restrictions, or business hours changes. The announcement date indicates when the announcement was made. The affected region describes the geographic area impacted. The announcement URL links to the detailed announcement page. Google can display special announcements prominently in search results, often with a highlighted appearance.

Key Properties

SpecialAnnouncement schema includes several important properties. The category property classifies the announcement type. The datePosted indicates when the announcement was published. The expires property specifies when the announcement is no longer relevant. The announcementsLocation describes affected locations. The webFeed property links to an RSS or Atom feed for updates. The newsUpdatesAndGuidelines property links to the primary information page. These properties help Google understand the scope and timeliness of your announcement.

COVID-19 and Public Health Use

During the COVID-19 pandemic, SpecialAnnouncement schema was widely used by government agencies, health organizations, schools, and businesses to communicate pandemic-related information. Google displayed these announcements prominently in search results with special formatting. The schema helped users find authoritative information about testing sites, vaccine availability, restrictions, and safety guidelines during a rapidly changing situation.

General Use Cases

Beyond public health, SpecialAnnouncement schema can be used for various urgent announcements. Businesses can announce temporary closures or reduced hours. Schools can announce emergency closures due to weather. Government agencies can announce service disruptions or policy changes. Event organizers can announce cancellations or postponements. Any time-sensitive announcement that affects the public can benefit from this schema type.

Best Practices

When using SpecialAnnouncement schema, keep your announcements current and accurate. Set the expires property to remove outdated information from search results. Update the schema when the situation changes. Link to authoritative sources for detailed information. Use clear, concise language that users can quickly understand. Monitor Google Search Console to ensure your announcements are being processed correctly.

Conclusion

SpecialAnnouncement schema is a powerful tool for communicating time-sensitive information to the public.
