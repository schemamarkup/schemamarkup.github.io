What Is FAQ Schema?

FAQ Schema is structured data that marks up a list of questions and answers on a webpage. When implemented correctly, Google can display your FAQs directly in search results as an interactive accordion. This rich result feature takes up significant search real estate and positions your content as the go-to source for answers.

When to Use It

Use FAQ Schema on any page that contains a list of frequently asked questions. Common applications include product pages, service pages, help centers, about pages, and landing pages.

Real-World Examples

Product Page: An ecommerce store adds FAQ Schema to their product pages answering common questions about shipping, returns, and sizing. The FAQs appear in search results, answering customer questions before they click, leading to higher conversion rates.

Use Cases

  • Ecommerce product pages with shipping and sizing questions
  • Service pages addressing common client concerns
  • Help centers and knowledge bases
  • Landing pages for lead generation
  • Local business pages with location and hours questions

Common Mistakes

Too few questions: Google requires at least 2 questions for FAQ rich results eligibility.

Non-factual content: FAQs should provide factual answers, not promotional content.

Hidden questions: All FAQ content must be visible to users on the page, not hidden behind tabs or accordions.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Include at least 3-5 questions for meaningful rich results
  • Use natural language questions that match real user search queries
  • Keep answers concise but complete (2-4 sentences)
  • Do not use FAQ Schema for promotional or advertising content
  • Test your markup with Google's Rich Results Test

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use FAQ Schema on any page?

Yes, as long as the page contains genuine FAQ content that is visible to users.

How many questions do I need?

At least 2 for Google to display as a rich result. More questions increase the value.

Will FAQ Schema affect my page loading speed?

Minimally. JSON-LD is lightweight and does not affect page speed or Core Web Vitals.

Can I update my FAQ Schema later?

Yes, simply update the markup on your page and Google will recrawl and update.

Does FAQ Schema work with voice search?

Yes, virtual assistants often pull answers from FAQ Schema for voice responses.

Is FAQ Schema a Google ranking factor?

Not directly, but it improves visibility and CTR, which indirectly benefits SEO.

Can I have both FAQ and Product Schema on the same page?

Yes, multiple schema types on the same page are supported and recommended.

What happens if my FAQ content violates Google's policies?

Google may remove the rich result or issue a manual action. Follow guidelines carefully.

Do I need to update FAQ Schema when I change the questions?

Yes, keep your markup in sync with the visible content on your page.

Can I use this tool for Q&A pages?

For Q&A pages, use QAPage schema instead. FAQ Schema is specifically for FAQ content.
