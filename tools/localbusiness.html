What Is Local Business Schema?

Local Business Schema is a specialized Schema.org type that provides search engines with detailed information about your physical business. It includes your business name, address, phone number, opening hours, geographic coordinates, and business category. This markup directly powers local search features that drive foot traffic and phone calls.

There are many subtypes including Restaurant, Salon, Doctor, Plumber, Lawyer, and more. Choosing the right type helps Google accurately categorize your business.

Features

  • Complete address markup with geolocation coordinates
  • Opening hours specification with weekday schedules
  • Phone number, website URL, and business description

When to Use It

Use Local Business Schema on any website that represents a physical business location. It is essential for restaurants, retail stores, medical practices, law firms, salons, plumbers, electricians, and any service business that serves a local area.

Common Mistakes

Wrong business type: Selecting a generic LocalBusiness type instead of a specific subtype misses opportunities for specialized rich results.

Inconsistent NAP: Your name, address, and phone must exactly match your Google Business Profile and website footer.

Missing coordinates: Without latitude and longitude, Google cannot accurately map your location.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Keep NAP data identical across your website, Google Business Profile, and schema markup
  • Include your service area if you travel to customers
  • Add opening hours for each day of the week, including holidays
  • Use high-quality images that represent your business
  • Test with Google's Rich Results Test after implementation

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need both LocalBusiness and Organization Schema?

LocalBusiness is a subtype of Organization. Use LocalBusiness for physical locations and Organization for corporate websites.

How long until I see local search improvements?

Typically 2-4 weeks for Google to process and display your enhanced results.

Can I mark up multiple locations?

Yes, create separate schema blocks for each location on their respective pages.

What if my business operates in multiple categories?

Choose the primary category that best describes your business.

Does this work for online-only businesses?

No, LocalBusiness is designed for businesses with a physical location. Use Organization Schema instead.
