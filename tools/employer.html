What Is Employer Review Schema?

Employer Review Schema uses the Organization type combined with AggregateRating to display employer ratings and review counts in search results. It includes your company name, URL, logo, description, contact information, physical address, and overall rating from employee reviews. This markup helps search engines present a complete picture of your organization to potential job candidates.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use this if I have reviews from Glassdoor or Indeed?

Yes, if you have permission to display the aggregate rating, you can include it in your schema markup.

What rating scale should I use?

Use a 1-5 scale where 5 is the best. Google displays ratings as star values in search results.

Do I need a minimum number of reviews?

There is no minimum, but a higher review count adds credibility to your displayed rating.

Is Employer Schema different from Organization Schema?

Employer reviews use the Organization schema type with an added AggregateRating. This generator simplifies the process.

Can I update my rating dynamically?

Yes, update the ratingValue and reviewCount in your schema markup whenever your ratings change.
