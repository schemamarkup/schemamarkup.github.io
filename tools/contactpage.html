Your contact page is one of the most important pages on your website. Contact Page Schema helps search engines understand exactly how customers can reach you by marking up your contact information. This structured data can power rich results that display your phone number, address, and email directly in search, making it effortless for potential customers to get in touch.

What Is Contact Page Schema?

Contact Page Schema is a subtype of WebPage specifically designed for contact and about pages. It includes properties like name, description, URL, author, image, and publication dates. When combined with Organization or LocalBusiness schema on the same page, it creates a complete picture of your business's contact information for search engines.

How It Works

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need Contact Page Schema in addition to Organization Schema?

Yes. Use ContactPage for the page type and Organization for the business details. They work together for complete coverage.

Can I use this for multiple location pages?

Yes. Create separate ContactPage schema blocks for each location on their respective pages.

Does this schema type show a contact form in search results?

Contact Page Schema helps Google understand your page but does not directly create interactive contact forms in search.

Is ContactPage a recommended schema type by Google?

Yes, ContactPage is a recognized WebPage subtype that helps search engines categorize your contact pages.
