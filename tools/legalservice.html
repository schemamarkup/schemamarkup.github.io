What Is Legal Service Schema?

Legal Service Schema is a subtype of LocalBusiness schema designed for legal professionals and law firms. It includes properties for practice name, areas of law, location, opening hours, pricing, and client ratings. Google uses this markup to display legal services in local search results with rich information that helps clients choose representation.
