What Is Job Posting Schema?

Job Posting Schema is structured data that describes a job opening. It includes the job title, description, hiring organization, location, salary, employment type, and posting dates. Google uses this markup to display job listings in Google for Jobs, the dedicated job search feature in Google Search.

Features

  • Job title, description, and company information
  • Location with city-level address
  • Salary range with currency and period (year, month, hour)
  • Employment type (full-time, part-time, contract, etc.)
  • Posting and expiration dates
  • Valid JSON-LD formatted for Google for Jobs

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Job Posting Schema guarantee Google for Jobs inclusion?

It is required but not guaranteed. Google must also approve your site for job listings.

Can I use this for remote positions?

Yes, use the jobLocation type to indicate remote work.
