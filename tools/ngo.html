What Is NGO Schema?

NGO Schema is a subtype of Organization schema designed specifically for non-governmental and non-profit organizations. It includes properties for organization name, mission description, logo, location, contact details, and social media profiles. Google uses this markup to display NGO information in search results and knowledge panels, helping supporters discover and engage with your cause.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between NGO and Organization schema?

NGO is a more specific subtype that helps search engines classify your organization as a non-governmental entity, improving relevance for cause-related searches.

Can I use this for my local charity?

Yes, NGO Schema works for organizations of all sizes, from local charities to international humanitarian organizations.

Should I include donation information in my schema?

While donation-specific properties aren't in NGO Schema, you can combine it with other schema types to highlight fundraising and donation opportunities.

How does NGO schema help with grant applications?

While not directly related to grants, a strong online presence with proper schema builds the organizational credibility that grant committees look for.

Can I add multiple social profiles?

Yes, include all your active social media profiles. This helps supporters connect with your organization across platforms.
