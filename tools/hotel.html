What Is Hotel Schema?

Hotel Schema is a subtype of LocalBusiness Schema specifically designed for hotels, resorts, and lodging properties. It includes properties like name, description, star rating, amenities, price range, address, telephone, and aggregate ratings. Google uses this markup to display rich hotel results with amenity icons, star ratings, and pricing information that help travelers compare and book.

Features

  • Hotel name, description, URL, and telephone
  • Complete address with city, state, ZIP, and country
  • Star rating for quality classification
  • Amenities checklist (pool, gym, wifi, parking, etc.)
  • Price range indicator for budget display
  • Hotel image for visual rich results
  • Aggregate rating and review count integration

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Hotel Schema for vacation rentals?

Vacation rentals are better marked up with LodgingBusiness schema. Hotel schema is for traditional hotel properties.

What star rating scale does Google use?

Star ratings typically range from 1 to 5 stars. Use the starRating field with the appropriate numeric value.

Do I need to list all amenities?

List the amenities you offer. Google uses these to display amenity icons in hotel search results.

Should schema match my online travel agent listings?

Yes, consistent information across your website, OTAs, and Google Business Profile builds trust with both search engines and travelers.
