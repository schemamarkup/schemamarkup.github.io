What Is PodcastEpisode Schema?

PodcastEpisode Schema is a specialized Schema.org type that describes an individual podcast episode. It includes the episode name, description, audio URL, image, publication date, duration, author, season and episode numbers, and the series name. This structured data powers podcast discovery across Google Search, Google Podcasts, and other podcast platforms that consume schema markup.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need separate schema for each episode?

Yes, each episode should have its own PodcastEpisode Schema on its respective page.

Is PodcastEpisode Schema different from AudioObject?

Yes. PodcastEpisode is specifically for podcast episodes and includes season, episode, and series metadata. AudioObject is more general.

Can I use this for video podcasts?

Video podcasts should use VideoObject Schema. PodcastEpisode is for audio-based podcast content.

How does Google use season and episode numbers?

Google uses these to organize episodes in series order and display context in search results.
