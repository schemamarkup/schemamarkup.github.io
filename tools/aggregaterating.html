What Is Aggregate Rating Schema?

Aggregate Rating Schema represents the overall rating of an item based on multiple individual ratings or reviews. It includes the average rating value, total rating count, and the rating scale. Google displays this as star ratings in search results, providing instant credibility and attracting more clicks.

Features

  • Average rating value and total count
  • Customizable rating scale (best/worst rating)
  • Review count support for detailed display
  • Item name and URL association
  • Valid JSON-LD format

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use AggregateRating without individual reviews?

Yes, if you have an overall rating from any source, you can use AggregateRating.

Do I need both Review and AggregateRating?

They are independent. Use AggregateRating for overall averages and Review for individual testimonials.
