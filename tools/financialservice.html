What Is Financial Service Schema?

Financial Service Schema is a subtype of LocalBusiness schema designed for financial services providers. It includes properties for business name, location, services offered, fee structure, and opening hours. Google uses this markup to display financial service providers in local search results with information that helps clients make informed decisions about financial matters.

Frequently Asked Questions

What financial services can I list?

Include all services your firm offers such as financial planning, investment management, tax preparation, accounting, insurance, or mortgage services.

Should I include fee information?

Yes, describing your fee structure helps set client expectations and attracts qualified leads who understand your pricing model.

Can I use this for online-only financial services?

Yes, Financial Service Schema works for both physical and online financial service providers.

Is this schema suitable for cryptocurrency services?

Financial Service Schema covers traditional financial services. For cryptocurrency-specific businesses, consider additional relevant schema types.

Do I need regulatory disclaimers in my schema?

While not required in schema markup, ensure your website includes all necessary regulatory disclosures for your financial services.
