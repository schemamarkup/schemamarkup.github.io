About Schema Markup Generator

Schema Markup Generator is a free online tool that helps website owners, SEO professionals, and developers create valid JSON-LD structured data quickly and easily.

Why We Built This

Writing schema markup by hand is error-prone and time-consuming. We wanted a tool that just works — no sign-ups, no ads, no fluff. Just pick a schema type, fill in the fields, and copy clean JSON-LD ready for your website.

What We Offer

  • 15+ schema types covering Business, Content, Ecommerce, Website, and Education
  • Real-time JSON-LD generation with instant validation
  • Copy, download, beautify, and minify options
  • Works entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server
  • Free to use forever, no account required
